Escape Road

    
242 4.14 5

Escape Road is an outstanding simulation racing game that immerses you in thrilling action as a bank robber driving a car to escape the city. You must carefully drive your automobile in order to escape safely via perilous streets and desolate places. Can you survive when police cars chase you everywhere?

How do I earn high points in the game?

In Escape Road, in addition to good driving, you must collect heaps of money spread around the route to earn points and purchase further stunning automobiles after each level. The game's highlight is the ability to drive in a 360-degree rotation. As a result, if you overlook a pile of money, do not hesitate to swiftly turn around and retrieve it. Remember, the most important thing is to avoid the police siege at all costs. You can crash into barriers and vehicles, but be careful not to get stuck. Be on the alert for corners where cops are arriving from all directions. Good luck!

How to control

  • Move left with A key or left arrow button
  • Move right with D key or right arrow button
